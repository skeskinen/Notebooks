====== 6 ======
Created maanantai 26 lokakuu 2020

Simplify living by adding structure to life.
People are suffering from lack of direction and structure. The meaninglessness makes them vulnerable to all sorts of toxic identities.
We should develop cultural technology that allows people to feel like they are meaningful part of a larger whole, i.e. role based society or apprenticeships.
