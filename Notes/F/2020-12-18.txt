====== Actions and passivity ======
You are an actor in a play. You don't get to choose your role but you should perform your role to the best of your ability. 

====== Wise man ======

Man who does what he wants to do is free.
Man who has more than he needs is rich.

Therefore a wise man is always free and always rich for he desires nothing and needs nothing.
