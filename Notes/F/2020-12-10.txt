====== Theory of mind ======

Part of early puberty is developing a working theory of mind. At this point people can understand that other people have memories, thoughts and other internal processes. They realize that if they do something it'll affect other people's opinion of them.


====== Hypernormal stimulus ======

All addictions have some hypernormal stimulus that drives the addiction. You can have hypernormal stimuli, but you need to have a mature relationship with them which most people won't have. The society is more healthy if it has less hypernormal stimuli.
