====== Collapse 101, Post-gloom ======

Life-centered society. Real progress must be positive to the nature as a whole, otherwise it will eventually undermine humanity.

Responsibility to the biosphere should be more fundamental than responsibility to humanity.

Responsibility and mortality. What should you do when you accept that your family, country and race will die?


====== Coping and indifference ======

To be truly indifferent towards a choice you should completely accept all outcomes.
