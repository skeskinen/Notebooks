Three levels of maturity:
* Able to take care of yourself without disrupting the society.
* Able to understand needs of the others, social situations and cause-effect relationships.
* Able to understand personal liimts and capabilities and act according to the needs of the society.

Working well in society is part of being a human. Even if you don't mature you can do well as an individual. But you aren't filling part of your Telos.
