====== Momentum ======
Created Wednesday 02 September 2020

Moves with good momentum are often followed by another momentum move. Momentum bets should follow the overall trend.
Momentum crossing the 0 point is an indicator for short term reversal. Momentum crossing 0 is often used as a buy/sell signal.
