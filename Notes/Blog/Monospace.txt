====== Monospace ======
Created Monday 12 October 2020

* Why simplicity matters
	* Make everything as simple as possible but not simpler
	* We live in VUCA world, simplicity is needed to balance things
* Why monospace is simpler than proportional fonts
	* Easy to render, easy to layout
	* Clear rules and structure
	* No possibility for complex kerning rules
	* Bugs in font code, possible security risk
* Why you need a high level goal before you can determine what is the simplest solution
* What is the high level goal of the blog
	* Serve as a medium for my writing
	* Communicate ideas as clearly and simply as possible
	* Communicate ideas with the most legible and understandable typeface
* Defend readability of monospace fonts
* Conclusion
	* I'm not reimplementing readers browsers so it's kind pointless.
	* But the sentiment matters.
	* Medium is the message
	* I stand proud behind my ideology
